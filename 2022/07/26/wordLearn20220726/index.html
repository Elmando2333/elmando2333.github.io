words

necessary

adj.必要的
n.生活必需品

eg: The necessary agro-technological tools to eradicate hunger.

eradicate: 连根拔起，铲除，消灭

  • evacuate 清空，撤空
  • extinguish 灭亡
    • extinguisher 灭火器

+necessity

马斯洛需求：Maslow’s hierarchy of needs

  1. Battery
  2. Wifi/5G Wireless Fidelity
  3. Survival , physiological needs
  4. Safety and Security
  5. Social Needs: friends,family
  6. Esteem 尊重
    • self-esteem,confidence,achievement
  7. Self-actualization 自我实现，实现自我价值
    • creativity, problem solving, Charity
    • Bill Gates, Buffett, naked donation(全部捐掉)
    • authenticity (真实), spontaneity (spontaneously,自发，自觉的)

essentail

adj. 基本的，本质的

eg: Be essential to proper functioning of captial markets.

。。。对于资本市场的正常运行必不可少。

essential needs

indispensable

adj. 不可分割，必不可少的

eg: For poor contries which prize growth over greenery, coal seems indispensable: cheap,abundant and reliable.
