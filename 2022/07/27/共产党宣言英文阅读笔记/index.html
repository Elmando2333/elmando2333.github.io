共产党宣言|英文阅读笔记（一）

Elmando
2022-07-27
思考, 英语, 读书

最近开始阅读英文版本的《共产党宣言》，中文版的在两年前的马哲课上浅看过几页，但因为是应付课程要求读的，多少有些不认真。这次再重新看一看，顺便提升一下相关领域的英文水平。

序言

Today, we face an unprecedented complex and changing world. To cope with this rapid changing world requires not only communication skills, but also adequate knowledge of cultures other than our own home culture.

Among the most important developments in present-day global culture is the ever increasing culture exchanges and understanding between different nations and people.

马克思创立的哲学思想为历史唯物主义，其最大的愿望是对于个人的全面而自由的发展。
